Family:

A family of droseraceae with sticky petals.

Habitat:

It can be found on the moon, especially on lunar mare.

Appearance:

Campanulas are a type of insectivorous with sticky petals. It has glowing look by reflecting the moonlight. Its petals keeps folding and unfolding like leaves of wood sorrels.

Key Characteristics:

  • It is used in psychiatric treatment by soothing anxiety.
  • It preys on butterflies.
  • It doesn’t need sunlight or water.
  • It’s a glass-like flower that reflects lights, thus its color is usually pale yellow which is the color of the moon.

What we know so far
The campanulas are the one and only creature living on the Moon. Campanulas don’t need sunlight or water to grow, thus they are susceptible to bright lights except moonlight. Thus, the butterflies evolved to flicker intermittently. Its scent is good for soothing anxiety, thus it’s used to treat mental disorders.
Guidlines for human interaction with this creature Human would interact with the campanula with butterflies by feeding it or just holding it until the butterfly flickers.
